# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Power BI Project)

An end-to-end Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance across regions, products, and sales channels.

This project demonstrates how raw data can be transformed into meaningful insights to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To identify key revenue drivers, analyze regional performance, and provide actionable insights for business growth.

---

## 🧩 Project Workflow

### 🔹 1. Data Collection
- Imported dataset from Excel/CSV

### 🔹 2. Data Cleaning (Power Query)
- Removed null values
- Standardized column formats
- Created calculated columns

### 🔹 3. Data Modeling
- Built relationships between tables
- Implemented a star schema approach

### 🔹 4. DAX Calculations
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Sales Trends

### 🔹 5. Dashboard Design
- KPI Cards (Revenue, Profit)
- Map Visualization (Regional Analysis)
- Line Charts (Monthly Trends)
- Pie Chart (Sales Channels)
- Bar Charts (Product Performance)

### 🔹 6. Storytelling with Data
Focused on converting insights into business decisions.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🌍 Regional Performance
- North America contributes ~40% of total revenue  
- Europe shows strong growth potential  

### 📦 Product Trends
- Electronics dominate revenue (~30%)  
- Apparel shows steady growth  

### 🛒 Sales Channels
- Online sales generate ~65% of total revenue  
- Offline channels provide higher profit margins  

### ⚡ Order Priorities
- High-priority orders significantly impact profitability  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand digital strategy in Europe  
- Focus on high-growth product categories  
- Balance online and offline channels  
- Optimize logistics for priority orders
